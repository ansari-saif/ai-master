**Objective**: Develop a minimal hospital management system with essential features for managing patients, doctors.

### **Requirements**

1. **Patient Management**
    - Add, update, view, and delete patient details:
        - Name
        - Age
        - Gender
        - Contact information

2. **Doctor Management**
    - Add, update, view, and delete doctor details:
        - Name
        - Specialty
        - Contact information

2. **Doctor Management**
    - Add, update, view, and delete doctor details:
        - Patient Name
        - Doctor Name
        - DateTime
