**Objective**: Develop a minimal hospital management system with essential features for managing patients, doctors, and assets.

### **Requirements**

1. **Patient Management**
    - Add, update, view, and delete patient details:
        - Name
        - Age
        - Gender
        - Contact information
2. **Doctor Management**
    - Add, update, view, and delete doctor details:
        - Name
        - Specialty
        - Contact information
3. **Asset Management**
    - Add, update, view, and delete hospital assets:
        - Asset name
        - Quantity
        - Location
